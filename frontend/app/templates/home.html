All questions in the quiz are based on specific Bible passages, ensuring a fair and focused experience for everyone. The three key passages you'll explore are:

By studying these passages, you'll delve into:

  • Genesis: The story of Joseph and the development of Israel.
  • Zechariah: Visions and messages from a lesser-known minor prophet.
  • Romans: Foundational Christian doctrines from Paul's letter to the Romans.
